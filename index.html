Who are we?

Established in the fall of 2005, Iconic-Zone Hotel has cemented her legacy one the best 4-star hotels in the wider East african region. A recipient of many hospitality awards, it's considered a must visit for all in who chance on Nairobi.

Capacity

Comprising of 100 Luxurious Premier apartments , 80 Premier Plus apartments and 80 Premier Suites apartments.The rooms were designed to reflect the history of the hotel grounds, which have a close tie to the old African glamour, with a touch of modernism.All accommodations are equipped with the latest conveniences and technologies to satisfy the hotels discerning guests, guaranteeing a convenient and memorable stay with this iconic hotel. These rooms all feature marble lavatories, large work desks with international plug ins, LCD TVs, room service and a fully stocked minibar.

Conference Facilities

The hotel has a number of versatile event spaces that can be used individually or in combination for larger spaces. All rooms have adjustable lighting, individual heating and cooling and built in AV features, with most spaces offering city views.Just a few steps off the main lobby, the event space opens to a majestic common area that can be used as a quiet retreat from a busy meeting, a breakout conversation or convenient registration area.
