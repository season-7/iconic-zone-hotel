Rooms

At Iconic Zone, we pride ourselves in providing the highest quality rooms.We have premium rooms, premium plus rooms and premium suites. Premium rooms feature a dual queen-sized beds and attached modern washrooms. Premium plus rooms feature a king-sized bed, a balcony and attached washrooms. Premium suites feature a king-sized bed, a sitting area,a balcony and a bathtub fitted washroom.

Outdoors

We know you can't keep indoors all day. At Iconic Zone, we offer state of the art facilities to keep you fit and healthy. We boost a modern fitness facility with trained instructors. We also have a full-sized pool to enjoy the warm temparatures of Nairobi. If you fancy running around then you can hit the modern tennis court during your stay here.

Lounge and Common Rooms

At Iconic Zone we ensure you enjoy your stay here. If you fancy a cold cocktail over a conversation, you will find yourself at home in our modern lounge. Maybe you want to kick back with your headphones buried in an enticing novel, the library is open 24hr everyday.Perharps you want to quickly put in some work, reply to an email or even catch-up with your favourite blog, our computer room is available.

Conference Facilities

For all your meeting needs, Iconic Zone has three modern and sophisticated conference rooms.These versatile event spaces that can be used individually or in combination for larger spaces. All rooms have adjustable lighting, individual heating and cooling and built in AV features, with most spaces offering city views.Just a few steps off the main lobby, the event space opens to a majestic common area that can be used as a quiet retreat from a busy meeting, a breakout conversation or convenient registration area.
